Rules

  1. Be nice to everyone and watch your language
  2. Respect the borders and other players if you come too close
  3. Do not randomly kill players
  4. NSFW buildings are not allowed
  5. Do not loot/grief other bases
  6. Do not use a Hacked/Modded* client.
  7. Trolling is allowed as long as it doesn't break any rules
  8. Do not ask for your items back if they were lost because of death
  9. Do not ask moderators for a tp, we are trying to use as few "cheats" as possible
  10. Duplication of items is not allowed**
  11. If you suspect that any other player is breaking any of these rules, you are required to contact the mods.

If any of these rules are broken you will join the "warned" team or get banned depending on what you did However, hacking/x-raying/x-ray resourse pack is insta ban


* It is okay to use graphic mods such as optifine or shaders. Mods that gives an advantage in the game such as a map or waypoints.


** It is okay to exploits glitches as long as no items are dublicated, this is a very thin line so be careful.

Other info

What is the server about?

The server exist to creat a community simmular to bigger servers such as hermit craft where creative players can join forces and play together. The main focus of the server is to create giant structures or bases but it is also fine to focus on more community projects such as minigames and economy.

How do I join the server?

it is really easy to join you just need to fill out this Google Form. However, we are quite picky when it comes to choose new players. To make it easier for yourself to join I would recomend you to join my Discord server or tune in for some of my streams and interact with me or the other owner.
