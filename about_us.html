Info

Thing is my first fursona I ever created and remains my main to this day. You have seen him all around this website because I use him as an avatar. Thing I currently a dutch angel dragon but this might change in the future as he have a few features that might not fit a dutchie


Lore

The lore isnt 100% done yet but I can tell you about a few things. So Thing made a deal with a deamon in order to have power and this is why he have a mark on his chest. The power made him enable to summon weapons depending on his needs and somewhat change his from. You can read more about the different forms he can take below. However, Thing regrets this deal and that is why you can see him crying sometimes. It is also easy for him to lose control while he uses his powers.
