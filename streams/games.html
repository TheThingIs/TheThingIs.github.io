Join The Realm!

You want to join the realm? Great! But there are a few thing you have to do.

  1. Join my discord server
  2. Read the rules!
    1. Be nice to everyone and watch your language
    2. Respect the borders and other players if you come to close
    3. Do not randomly kill players
    4. No nsfw buildings
    5. Do not loot/grief other bases
    6. No hacking
    7. Trolling is allowed as long as it doesn't break any rules
    8. Do not ask for your items back bc of a death, unless it is a glitch death.
    9. Do not ask moderators for a tp
    • If any of these rules are broken you will join the "warned" team or get banned depending on what you did
    • However, hacking/x-raying/x-ray resourse pack is insta ban
  3. Fill in this Google Form
  4. I will contact you if you get to enter the realm or not
