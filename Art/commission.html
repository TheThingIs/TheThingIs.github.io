Full body

Price : 10$
Size : 1920*1080 pixels
Drawing time : 3-5 hours

Info
Full drawings are full body pictures exept the legs usually. Most of them are based on Growtopia sets but I would love to do furries and other drawings too. They take quite some time to make and are usually streamed via my Twitch. If you want to infuense the drawing I would recomend you to be there during the stream. I usually send PM's with my sketch to make sure you are happy with it too before I start with line art and colouring.

Options

Backround: Transparent, Shapes, one Colour, a Gradiant or a Growtopia backgound
Name: Yes or No
Streaming: I can stream or I can't stream
Pose: I usually do my own poses but you can specify by sending me an existing pose
