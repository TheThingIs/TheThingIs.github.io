Head / Bust

Price : 5$
Size : 500*500 pixels
Drawing time : 2-3 hours

Info

Head drawings are usually used for profile pictures and have the size 500 by 500 pixels. The Drawing includes the head, neck and sometimes the top part of the bust. I usually base the heads on furties or Growtopia sets but I would love to test other things too. The backgound can be transparent, simpel chape or full colour.

Full body

Price : 10$
Size : 1920*1080 pixels
Drawing time : 3-5 hours

Info

Full drawings are full body pictures exept the legs usually. Most of them are based on Growtopia sets but I would love to do furries and other drawings too. They take quite some time to make and are usually streamed via my Twitch. If you want to infuense the drawing I would recomend you to be there during the stream. I usually send PM's with my sketch to make sure you are happy with it too before I start with line art and colouring.

Options

Background: Transparent, Shapes, one Colour, a Gradiant or a Growtopia backgound
Name: Yes or No
Streaming: I can stream or I can't stream
Pose: I usually do my own poses but you can specify by sending me an existing pose

Price (1-2 characters): 15$
Price (3-4 characters): 20$
Size : 1920*1080 pixels
Drawing time : 4-7 hours

Info

Complex drawings are extremly fun to make but takes a lot of time planing. Usually You or I choose a feature of the Persona (works best with Growtopia sets) and base the entire drawing around that. It can also be an action between two or more characters. This is for when you want more then just the persona of your choise or multible characters. They usually tell a story and that is why it takes so long to make.
